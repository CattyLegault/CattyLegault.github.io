About Algonquin College

Algonquin College is an applied arts and technology college located in the national captial region of Ottawa. It is known for its hands on programs that help current and future stuents get into their desired careers. There are three Algonquin campuses, Ottawa being the main campus, Perth and Pembroke.

Originally built in 1957 on top of occupied first nations land, the college has over 19,000 students and a total of 180+ programs. All of their programs are focused in arts and technology. This makes Algonquin college the third largest college in Canada

Algonquin College strives on innovation and technology to get students on the right path to achieve their desiered career. The college works with talented and educated professionals that teach new and current students the fundamentals of technology and research.
