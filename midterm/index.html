Why choose Algonquin College?

Algonquin College is one of the few applied arts and technology colleges located in the national capital region of Ottawa, ON. Algonquin College strives in hands-on learning for current and future students. It is an english learning college that is publicly funded and has two other locations; Perth, and Pembroke. The college has a wide range of programs that heavily focus on student sucess. Offering flexible learning options from on-site all the way to remote, this gives student free range to learn anywhere. Algonquin College is also very diverse, they are recongnized for its multicultural environment. Overall, this makes Algonquin College one of the largest colleges in Canada.

Facts about Algonquin College!

  1. Algonquin College was established in 1967 and it was named after the First Nations people that occupied the Ottawa Valley.
  2. The college has over 155 programs, 18 apprenticeships, and 40 co-op programs
